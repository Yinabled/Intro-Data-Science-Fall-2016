Introduction to Data Science

Columbia University Science Honor Program Fall 2016

Welcome to The Class!

In this class, we will explore theories in Probability, Statistics and Machine Learning, and apply them to solve real data problems using the programming language R. We will build the foundations from scratch --- no actual prerequisites required, but bring your curiosity! We will take your analytical skills to the next level. If you're someone seeking for challenges, we have something for you too!

Once again, welcome and enjoy.
